Armor grants you its Armor Rating if yours was previously lower. If your Might is not at least equal to the score shown, you are [[Restrained]] by wearing it.

Equipping or unequipping Armor requires a Scene (10 minutes) to complete.

| Type   | Rating | Value | Slots | Might |
| ------ | ------ | ----- | ----- | ----- |
| Light  | 1      | 50s   | 2     | 3     |
| Medium | 2      | 1g    | 2     | 4     |
| Heavy  | 3      | 2g    | 3     | 5     |
