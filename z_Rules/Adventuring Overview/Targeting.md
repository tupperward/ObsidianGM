---
name: Targeting
type: rule
---
Anything subjected to an effect is a Target. In order to be a viable Target, it must be perceivable.

### Distance

| Distance | Measurement |
|----------|-------------|
| Close | Within 5' |
| Near | Between 5 to 30' |
| Far | Beyond 30' |

Distance terms are used to quickly determine how far an effect can choose a viable Target.
