---
name: Statuses
type: rule
---
Statuses may occur as a result of magic, features, attacks, or other effects.

**[[Berserk]]:** It can't take the Cast Action or Focus, it can't be [[Frightened]], and it doesn't make Morale Checks.

**[[Blinded]]:** It can't see and is [[Vulnerable]].

**[[Burning]]:** A die roll indicates damage it takes at the start of its Turns, unless an appropriate action is taken to end it (such as dousing fire).

**[[Charmed]]:** It can't willingly attack the charmer.

**[[Confused]]:** Its Checks and [[Saves]] have Hinder, and [[Saves]] against its Actions have Favor.

**[[Dazed]]:** It can't Focus or Move unless it uses an Action to do so.

**[[Fatigued]]:** Fatigue is measured from 0 to 5. With 1 or more Fatigue, the following rules apply:
- Each Fatigue occupies an Item Slot.
- At 3 or more Fatigue, it can't Rush.
- At 5 Fatigue, it dies.

**[[Frightened]]:** It has a -2 penalty to damage it deals.

**[[Incapacitated]]:** The following rules apply:
- It can't Focus or use its Actions or Move.
- It fails all Might and Dexterity Checks.
- It is [[Vulnerable]].

**[[Invisible]]:** It can't be seen by normal senses, and those that can't see it act as [[Blinded]] for Checks and [[Saves]] involving it.

**[[Paralyzed]]:** It is [[Incapacitated]], and its Speed is 0.

**[[Prone]]:** You can either drop or stand from [[Prone]] by using 10' of Speed. While [[Prone]], the following rules apply:
- It can only move by crawling (2' of Speed for 1' of movement) and it can't Rush.
- It is [[Vulnerable]] for the purposes of [[Melee]] Attacks and Dodge Checks.

**[[Restrained]]:** It is [[Vulnerable]], and its Speed is 0.

**[[Sickened]]:** The following rules apply:
- It has a -2 penalty to any healing it receives.
- Additional effects may be indicated in parentheses, which apply while it is [[Sickened]].

**[[Suffocating]]:** Beings begin [[Suffocating]] after not breathing for 1 Minute.
- **Heroes:** Each Round while [[Suffocating]], roll a d8. If the result is equal to or greater than their Might, they gain 1 Fatigue.
- **Enemies:** Each Round while [[Suffocating]], they gain 1 Fatigue.

**[[Unconscious]]:** The following rules apply:
- It is [[Blinded]], [[Incapacitated]], and [[Prone]].
- Close Attacks [[Targeting]] it always Crit.

**[[Vulnerable]]:** Its attacks and its [[Saves]] against attacks have Hinder. Attacks targeting it and [[Saves]] against its attacks have Favor.
