**HD**: 4 (18 HP)
**Speed:** 80'
**Zone**: Midline
**Morale:** 7
**\# Appearing:** 1
**Senses**: Allsight, Telepathy
**Armor**: 3 [as (+1) Chain]
**Weak**: Cold Iron Weapons

Combo: 2×Hoof and 1×Horn
Hoof [Melee Attack]: 4 (d8)
Horn [Melee Attack]: 4 (d8)
Teleport [Cast] (1/Day): Teleports itself and any Being mounting it up to Far
