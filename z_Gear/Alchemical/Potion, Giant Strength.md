**Type:** Potion

For 1 Hour, the consumer gains these benefits:
- Their attacks deal an extra die of damage.
- They treat boulders as [[Melee]] [[Weapons]] with the Thrown Property (3d6 damage).

A Being can benefit from no more than one of these potions at a time.
