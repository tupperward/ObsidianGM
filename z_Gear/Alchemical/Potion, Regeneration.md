**Type:** Potion

The consumer of the item regains HP equal to the roll of a countdown die. The size of the die is determined by its Rank, as shown below.

| Rank | Die | Value |
|------|-----|-------|
| Regeneration I | Cd4 | 5g |
| Regeneration II | Cd6 | 10g |
| Regeneration III | Cd8 | 100g |
