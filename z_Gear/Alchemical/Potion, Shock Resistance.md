**Type:** Potion

For 1 Hour, the consumer gains these benefits:
- They gain a +2 bonus to [[Saves]] against being [[Dazed]], [[Paralyzed]], and Shock-based attacks and magic.
- They reduce any Shock damage they take by 1 per die used for the damage.
