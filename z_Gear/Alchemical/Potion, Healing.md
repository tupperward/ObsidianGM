**Type:** Potion

The consumer regains HP equal to a number of d6 rolls determined by the Potion's Rank, plus a modifier equal to its Rank:

| Rank | Roll | Value |
|------|------|-------|
| Healing I | d6+1 | 2g 50s |
| Healing II | 2d6+2 | 25g |
| Healing III | 3d6+3 | 250g |
