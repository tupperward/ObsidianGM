**Type:** Potion

For 1 Hour, the consumer's body becomes a cloud of gas and the following rules apply:
- They immediately drop anything they have equipped, and can't equip anything.
- They can Move by seeping into tiny spaces.
- They can't Attack or Cast.
- They are Immune to all damage from attacks that aren't made by Relics.
