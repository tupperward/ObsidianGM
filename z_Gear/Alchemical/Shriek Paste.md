**Type:** Concoction

The paste can coat either a 5-foot-square area or a [[Medium]] or smaller Being for as long as 1 week. When light is shed Near the paste, the paste rapidly crystallizes and emits a shriek audible within 300' for 1 minute. If the light moves out of range, the shriek ends at the end of the Turn. Afterward, the paste is inert.
