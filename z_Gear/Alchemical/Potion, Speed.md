This thin blue liquid tightens the consumer's muscles. For 1 Hour, the consumer's Speed is increased by 5' per Rank, as shown below:

| Rank | Speed Bonus | Value |
|------|-------------|-------|
| Speed I | +5' | 2g 50s |
| Speed II | +10' | 7g 50s |
| Speed III | +15' | 25g |
