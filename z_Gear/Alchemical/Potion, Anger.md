**Type:** Potion

The consumer becomes [[Berserk]] for a number of Rounds equal to the roll of a countdown die. The size of the die is determined by its Rank, as shown below.

| Rank | Die | Value |
|------|-----|-------|
| Anger I | Cd4 | 2g 50s |
| Anger II | Cd6 | 25g |
| Anger III | Cd8 | 250g |
