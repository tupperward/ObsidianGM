Havoc-wagers known to delight in chaos, goblins are smaller humanoids of a typically green, yellow, grey, or bluish skin color. This instinctual delight for chaos often puts them at-odds with the codes and laws of other societies, but goblin ingenuity can be an indispensable asset.
