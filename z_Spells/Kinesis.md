---
type: spell
name: "Kinesis"
damageBase: "Blunt"
---
You shoot an unsecured Target of 1 or less HD or Slots at a Target you can see within Near. If it strikes a Target, it hits them harmlessly unless this is Cast to deal damage.

You can spend Mana to increase the Targetable HD or Slots by 1 per Mana spent to do so for that Casting.
