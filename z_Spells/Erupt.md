---
type: spell
name: "Erupt"
damageBase: "Fire"
---
Magma coats the nearest surface to the Target for the duration (minimum 5-foot square), causing it to be Difficult Terrain that deals d6 first time a Being enters the Area on a Turn and when it ends its Turn in the Area.

Crit: Targets [[Burning]] (Cd4 while in the Area).
