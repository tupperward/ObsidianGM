---
type: spell
name: "Goop"
damageBase: "Acid"
---
A glob of sticky, acidic sludge coats the nearest surface to the Target (minimum 5-foot square) for the duration, causing it to be Difficult Terrain.

If also Cast to deal damage, the caustic acid causes those in the Area to be [[Burning]] (d6).

Crit: Targets in the Area are also [[Restrained]].
