---
type: spell
name: "Terraform"
damageBase: "Blunt"
---
You manipulate the loose earth or stone around the Target in the following ways:

**Area:** You can manipulate terrain in the Area, either causing it to be Difficult Terrain or a Structure that can fit within the area of effect of the Cast for the duration. This magic can't move any terrain that supports an Object.

**Being or Object:** Close earth or stone is Difficult Terrain for the duration.
