name: Finesse
---
Coordination and subtlety. Used to pickpocket, lockpick, and attack with Finesse [[Weapons]].

**Attack:** With Finesse [[Weapons]].

**Pickpocket:** Check to steal or plant one unsecured Item of 1 Slot or less.

**Lockpick:** Check to pick a lock, requiring a lockpick.
