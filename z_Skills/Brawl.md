name: Brawl

Exerting physical force. Used for Grapples, Shoves, and attacks with Brawl [[Weapons]].

**Attack:** With Brawl [[Weapons]].

**Grapples and Shoves:** You can try to grapple or shove Beings that are your Size or smaller.
- **Grapple:** Grappling rules appear in [[Position]] in Adventuring Overview.
- **Shove:** On a pass, you either shove it 5' or [[Prone]]. You must have a Brawl or Shield Weapon Equipped.
