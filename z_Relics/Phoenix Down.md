**Lore:** Relic Furs (Consumable)

This item can be placed on the corpse of a dead Being to return them to life at full HP. This does not work on Constructs or Undead. The Phoenix Down is consumed in this process.

**Powers:** Resurrection (one use)

**Value:** 2500g
