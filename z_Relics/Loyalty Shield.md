**Lore:** Fabled Relic Standard Shield (Bound)

Standard Shield that a Being must Bind themselves to to access its Powers, which are as follows:
- **(+1) Shield:** Grants a +1 bonus to [[Armor]].
- **Loyalty:** Can be thrown to attack. It then magically returns to the Wielder's hand after the attack.

**Value:** 1000g
