**Lore:** Fabled Relic Warhammer (Bound)

Warhammer that must be Bound to to access its Powers, which are as follows:
- **(+3) Bonus:** Deals an extra +3 damage.
- **Giants Bane:** Deals an extra damage die to giants.
- **Earth Shaker:** (1/Day) Strike the earth to cause all Near foes to be [[Dazed]] (d4 Rounds).
- **Loyalty:** Can be thrown to attack. It then magically returns to the Wielder's hand after the attack.
- **Sense Valuables:** Senses gold and gems within Near.
- **Thrown Ace:** Deals an extra damage die with thrown attacks.

**Value:** 13000g
