**Lore:** Fabled Relic Revolver

Revolver which silvers all bullets fired from it. It has the following Powers:
- **(+2) Weapon:** Deals an extra +2 damage.
- **Silvering:** Attacks with it count as Silvered.
- **Personality:** Will only fire at hostile foes.

**Value:** 5000g
