**Lore:** Relic [[Light]] [[Armor]]

A cloak of harpy's feathers. It acts as [[Light]] [[Armor]] and the feathers can be used as Daggers. It has the following Powers:
- **(+1) [[Armor]]:** Grants a +1 bonus to [[Armor]].
- **Infinite ((+1) Dagger):** The feathers it creates attack as (+1) Daggers. They become mundane feathers after the attack is made, and the feather grows back instantly.

**Value:** 1150g
