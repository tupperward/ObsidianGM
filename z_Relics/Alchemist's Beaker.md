**Lore:** Relic Beaker (Bound)

A beaker with three separate layers, each with the following Power:
- **[[Prima Materia]]:** When the Ritual to Bond with it is performed, the Being can provide up to three alchemical items and assign them to a layer. The beaker can be used to provide a use of that alchemical item, which replenishes automatically after 1 Day.

**Value:** 3000g
