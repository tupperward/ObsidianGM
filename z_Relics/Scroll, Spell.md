**Lore:** Relic Parchment (Consumable)

A Spell Scroll can be read to cast the spell from it as specified. Doing so costs no Mana and causes the Scroll to vaporize.

**Value:** 5g+5g per Mana that would be required to otherwise Cast the Spell
