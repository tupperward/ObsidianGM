**Lore:** Relic Dagger (Bound)

Dagger that a Being must Bind themselves to access its Powers, which are as follows:
- **Blinking:** They can use their Action or skip their Move to teleport to the Item if it is within Far.
- **Loyalty:** They can will it to return to their hand (no Action).

**Value:** 3000g
