---
type: perk
name: "Inspiring Presence"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: PRS 4+"
  - "Trained: Leadership"
---
If any of your Allies are [[Berserk]], [[Charmed]], [[Confused]], or [[Frightened]] by an effect that ends on a Countdown die, you can use your Action to make a [[Leadership]] Check to rally them. If you pass, the Countdown die for the effect decreases by 1 size.
