---
type: perk
name: "Guardian Angel"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: AWR 4+"
  - "Trained: Melee or Ranged"
---
Once per Round, if one of your Allies is Targeted by an Enemy, you can spend 1 [[Luck]] to make an attack against that Enemy. If you hit, your Ally has Favor on any Save forced by that Enemy this Turn.
