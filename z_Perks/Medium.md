---
type: perk
name: "Medium"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: AWR 4+"
  - "Trained: Mysticism"
---
You can conduct a 10-minute Ritual to ask up to 3 questions from the GM which can only be answered with a "yes" or "no." The Fates are the source of the information, and will answer these questions truthfully (if possible). Afterward, you can't conduct this Ritual until you complete a Quest.
