---
type: perk
name: "Combat Medic"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: RSN 4+"
  - "Trained: Medicine"
---
As an Action, you can tend to a willing Being's injuries. Doing so removes the [[Sickened]] Status if they have it, and they regain (d6 + your Reason) HP. That Being can't be affected by this Ability for the rest of the Shift.
