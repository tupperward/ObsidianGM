---
type: perk
name: "Scrapper's Delight"
prerequisites:
  - "Trained: Arcana & Craft"
---
You can conduct a 10-minute Ritual to magically break down an unsecured Item you touch throughout the ritual. The Item must be nonmagical, it must have a value of at least 5s, and it can occupy no more than 2 Slots. The Item becomes raw materials of a value equal to 5s per 1 Slot it previously occupied.
