---
type: perk
name: "Provoker"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: PRS 4+"
  - "Trained: Influence"
---
You can use your Action to intimidate or otherwise goad an Enemy that can see or hear you. When you do, your Allies make all [[Saves]] provoked by that Enemy's Attacks with Favor until the start of your Group's next Turn.
