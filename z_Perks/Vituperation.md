---
type: perk
name: "Vituperation"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: PRS 4+"
  - "Trained: Influence"
---
You can use your Action or skip your Move to rebuke an Enemy, and make an [[Influence]] Check with a penalty equal to the Target's Morale. If you pass, that Enemy is [[Vulnerable]] until your next Turn or until it deals damage. Beings without Morale can't be affected by this Ability.
