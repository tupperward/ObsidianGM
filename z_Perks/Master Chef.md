---
type: perk
name: "Master Chef"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: PRS 4+"
  - "Trained: Survival"
---
During a Scene where you have cooking tools, you can cook meals using 5s worth of Materials. Doing so makes d6+1 rations which restore d6 HP to anyone who eats one as part of a Breather. They function as normal rations after a Shift.
