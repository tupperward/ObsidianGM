---
type: perk
name: "Cardistry"
prerequisites:
  - "Spell: Any"
  - "Stat: DEX 4+"
---
You can use a deck of cards as a Trinket and can make attacks with cards drawn from a deck using your Cast Skill. The Deck must be used as a 2H Grip Weapon to do so. It deals d4, and has the [[Finesse]] and Thrown properties. Cards used for an attack magically reappear in the deck.
