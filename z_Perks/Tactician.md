---
type: perk
name: "Tactician"
prerequisites:
  - "Stat: RSN 4+"
  - "Trained: Leadership"
---
You can use your Action or skip your Move to issue a command, choosing from the following list. The command you issue lasts for the [[Combat]] or until you issue another command:
- **Attack:** Choose an Enemy. Attack and Cast Checks against it ignore Hinder.
- **Defend:** You and your Allies' [[Saves]] against attacks can't be Hindered.
- **Retreat:** Choose an Enemy, your Allies can Move an extra 10' if they end a Move further away from that Enemy.
