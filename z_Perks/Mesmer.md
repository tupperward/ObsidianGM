---
type: perk
name: "Mesmer"
prerequisites:
  - "Spell: Charm"
---
If you pass a Cast Check by 10 or more to cause a Target to be [[Charmed]] or [[Frightened]], you can also cause it to act in one of the following ways in a manner of your choice on its next Turn:
- Move up to half its Speed.
- Attack a Target (it can attack itself).
- Drop or pick up an Item.
