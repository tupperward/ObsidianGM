> *Bards were terrible at keeping secrets. They insisted on putting them to music.*
> --Tanya Huff, Sing the Four Quarters

Bard's are heralds of the awe-inspiring beauty of creation. While the painter and sculptor decorate space, a Bard decorates time.

**Iconic Bards**
- Edward Chris von Muir, Final Fantasy IV
- Jaskier, The Witcher
- Kvothe, The Kingkiller Chronicle
- Sylvando, Dragon Quest XI

## Playing Bards

The bard is a mid-defense, high-support class with a focus on optimizing your Group's d20 rolls. It's recommended that you avoid melee on most Bard builds.

**Action:** [[Virtuoso]]
**Move:** Midline/Backline
**Starting Pack:** Musician
**Key Stat:** Presence
**Complexity:** HHIII

**Training**

## Features
---
### [[Virtuoso]]
*Bard: 1st Level*
You can use your Action or skip your Move to make a [[Performance]] Check. If you pass, your Group gains one of the following benefits of your choice for that Round:

**Inspiration:** d6 bonus to Healing rolls
**Resolve:** Favor on [[Saves]]
**[[Valor]]:** Favor on Attack and Cast Checks

### [[Well-Versed]]
*Bard: 1st Level*
You ignore Prerequisites for Perks, and gain a Perk of your choice.

### [[Song of Rest]]
*Bard: 2nd Level*
During a Breather, you and your Allies gain a Studied die and regain additional HP equal to (your Presence + your Bard Level).

### [[Starstruck]]
*Bard: 4th Level, 10th Level*
When you perform [[Virtuoso]], you can choose a Near Enemy who hears the performance and make a [[Performance]] Check. If you pass, you can choose one of the following [[Statuses]] that affects it for Cd4 Rounds: [[Berserk]], [[Charmed]], [[Confused]], [[Frightened]].

When you become a 10th Level Bard, this Feature can affect all Near Enemies.

### [[Bravado]]
*Bard: 6th Level*
Your Will [[Saves]] can't be Hindered while you aren't [[Incapacitated]], and you can ignore effects that rely on you hearing them to be affected (such as a banshee's scream).

### [[Climax]]
*Bard: 8th Level*
Favor and bonus dice you grant can Explode.
