*[[Witch]]: 1st Level*

You gain a Perk with the Trained: [[Mysticism]] Prerequisite, and you can Cast Spells using [[Mysticism]].

**Spells:** You learn 4 Spells. You learn 1 other Spell every 2 [[Witch]] Levels hereafter.

**Mana:** Your Maximum Mana is equal to (4 x your [[Witch]] Level), and the highest amount of Mana you can spend to Cast a Spell is equal to (Awareness + half your [[Witch]] Level, round up). You regain spent Mana when you [[Rest]].
