*[[Luminary]]: 1st Level*

You can Cast Spells using [[Mysticism]].

**Spells:** You learn 4 Spells, two of which must always be [[Life]] and [[Light]]. You learn 1 other Spell every 2 [[Luminary]] Levels hereafter.

**Mana:** Your Maximum Mana is equal to (4 x your [[Luminary]] Level), and the highest amount of Mana you can spend to Cast a Spell is equal to (Awareness + half your [[Luminary]] Level, round up). You regain spent Mana when you [[Rest]].
