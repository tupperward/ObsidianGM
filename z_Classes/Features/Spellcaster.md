*[[Wizard]]: 1st Level*

You can Cast Spells using [[Arcana]].

**Spells:** You learn 4 Spells. You learn 1 other Spell every 2 [[Wizard]] Levels hereafter.

**Mana:** Your Maximum Mana is equal to (4 x your [[Wizard]] Level), and the highest amount of Mana you can spend to Cast a Spell is equal to (Reason + half your [[Wizard]] Level, round up). You regain spent Mana when you [[Rest]] or Study.
