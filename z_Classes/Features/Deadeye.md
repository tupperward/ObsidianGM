---
name: Deadeye
type: feature
class: Gunslinger
---
*[[Gunslinger]]: 1st Level*

After you pass a [[Ranged]] Check, you Crit on subsequent [[Ranged]] attacks on a d20 roll 1 lower, but no lower than 17. This resets to 0 at the end of your Turn if you didn't pass a [[Ranged]] Check since your last Turn.
