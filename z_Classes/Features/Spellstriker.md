*[[Magus]]: 1st Level*

You gain the [[Gish]] Perk and can Cast Spells using [[Arcana]].

**Spells:** You learn 2 Spells, one of which must always be [[Ward]]. You learn 1 other Spell every 3 [[Magus]] Levels hereafter.

**Mana:** Your Maximum Mana is equal to (2 x your [[Magus]] Level), and the highest amount of Mana you can spend to Cast a Spell is equal to (Reason + half your [[Magus]] Level, round up). You regain spent Mana when you [[Rest]].
