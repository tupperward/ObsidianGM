Keepers of the old way, hunters are survival experts that specialize in exploiting their enemies' weaknesses.

**Iconic Hunters**
- Aloy, Horizon
- Aragorn, The Lord of the Rings
- Samus Aran, Metroid
- San, Princess Mononoke

## Playing Hunter

The hunter is a low defense, high-crit striker. They can be built for [[Melee]] or [[Ranged]] positions.

**Action:** Attack
**Move:** Midline or Backline
**Starting Pack:** [[Scout]] or Warrior
**Key Stat:** Awareness
**Complexity:** HHIII

**Training**
**[[Weapons]]:** [[Melee]], [[Ranged]]
**Skills:** [[Survival]] and 4 others

## Features
---
### [[Hunter's Mark]]
*Hunter: 1st Level*
You can mark a Being when you attack it, or by skipping your Move if you can sense it. When you do, the following rules apply:
- You must Focus on the mark.
- When you attack it, roll two d20s and use the highest for the Check.

### [[Survivalist]]
*Hunter: 1st Level*
You gain the [[Padfoot]] Perk, you have Favor on Checks to track and navigate, and you can Forage while Traveling at a Normal Pace.

### [[Rover]]
*Hunter: 2nd Level*
Difficult Terrain doesn't impede your walking Speed, and you have Climb and Swim.

### [[Overwatch]]
*Hunter: 4th Level*
Your additional d20 for attacks with your [[Hunter's Mark]] also applies to your [[Saves]] provoked by the marked Target.

### [[Quarry]]
*Hunter: 6th Level*
You can sense Beings within Far as if by Blindsight if they are missing any HP or that are marked by your [[Hunter's Mark]].

### [[Lethal Precision]]
*Hunter: 8th Level*
You now roll three d20s with your [[Hunter's Mark]] and [[Overwatch]] Features and use the highest result.

### [[Apex Predator]]
*Hunter: 10th Level*
Damage you deal to the Target of your [[Hunter's Mark]] ignores Immune and [[Armor]].
