> *Man takes up the sword in order to shield the small wound in his heart sustained in a far-off time beyond remembrance.*
> --Kentaro Miura, [[Berserk]]

Students of the art of violence, fighters are defined by their weapon skills, and variety of martial techniques.

**Iconic Fighters**
- Beowulf, English mythology
- Gilgamesh, Mesopotamian legend
- Sir Lancelot, Arthurian legend

## Playing Fighter

The fighter is a mid-defense striker. In combat, get within effective distance for offense and prioritize the Attack Action.

**Action:** Attack
**Move:** Flexible
**Starting Pack:** [[Gladiator]], [[Knight]], Warrior, or [[Watchman]]
**Key Stat:** Might or Awareness
**Complexity:** HIIII

**Training**
**[[Weapons]]:** [[Melee]], [[Ranged]]
**Skills:** [[Brawl]] or [[Finesse]], and 3 from either [[Brawl]], [[Detect]], [[Finesse]], [[Leadership]], or [[Sneak]]

## Features
---
### [[Fighting Style]]
*Fighter: 1st Level*
You gain the [[Situational Awareness]] Perk and another Perk with the [[Melee]] or [[Ranged]] Training Prerequisite, ignoring prerequisites for this Perk.

### [[Valor]]
*Fighter: 1st Level, 4th Level, 8th Level*
The roll required for you to Crit on Attack Checks and [[Saves]] to Dodge or Block Attacks is reduced by 1, and is reduced by 1 more when you reach 4th and 8th Levels in this Class.

### [[Momentum]]
*Fighter: 2nd Level*
If you pass a Save against an attack, the next attack you make before the end of your next Turn is Favored.

### [[Muster for Battle]]
*Fighter: 6th Level*
You have two Actions on your first Turn.

### [[Harrying]]
*Fighter: 10th Level*
You can attack twice with the Attack Action, rather than just once.
