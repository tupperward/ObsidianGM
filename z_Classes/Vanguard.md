Vanguards are terrifying sentinels of heavy metal armor that use shields to devastate their foes, standing tall as mobile bastions of fortitude and might.

**Iconic Vanguards**
- Groot, Guardians of the Galaxy

- Reinhardt, [[Overwatch]]

## Playing Vanguard

The vanguard is a high-defense tank, trading off raw damage output for control. Actively defend through the Rush Action and Shoves.

**Action:** Shove (Attack)
**Move:** Frontline
**Starting Pack:** [[Sentinel]]
**Key Stat:** Might
**Complexity:** HHHII

**Training**
**[[Weapons]]:** [[Melee]]
**Skills:** [[Brawl]], and 3 from either [[Detect]], [[Leadership]], [[Medicine]], or [[Survival]]

## Features
---
### [[Stalwart]]
*Vanguard: 1st Level*
You gain the [[Protector]] Perk and, when you take the [[Hold]] Action, you can use the held Action or Move as late as the end of your next Turn.

### [[Guard]]
*Vanguard: 1st Level*
Once per Round, you can try to Shove a Close Target (no Action) when they become Close to you, or if you successfully Block their Attack.

### [[Rampant Charge]]
*Vanguard: 2nd Level*
You can push Targets you Shove ahead of you while you Move during the same Turn, shoving it [[Prone]] when you stop or push it into an occupied space. If you push it into an occupied space, it deals your Weapon's damage to the Target and whatever occupied the space.

### [[Wall]]
*Vanguard: 4th Level, 8th Level*
You are considered Large for Shoves. When you become an 8th Level Vanguard, you are considered Huge for Shoves.

### [[Unstoppable]]
*Vanguard: 6th Level*
If you use [[Rampant Charge]] and push a Being into another, you can attempt to push the additional Being ahead of you as well.

### [[Indestructible]]
*Vanguard: 10th Level*
While you aren't [[Incapacitated]] and have an [[Armor]] Rating of 1 or more, you are Immune to attack damage.
